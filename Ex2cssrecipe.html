Recipe for Grilled Cheese Sandwitch

list of Ingredients

  • 8 slices bacon

  • Â¼ cup butter, softened

  • 8 slices white bread

  • 8 slices American cheese

  • 8 slices tomato

Instructions

  1. Place bacon in a large, deep skillet.
  2. Cook over medium high heat until evenly brown. Drain, and set aside.
  3. Heat a large skillet over medium heat. Spread butter onto one side of each slice of bread.
  4. Lay 4 slices of bread, butter side down, in the skillet. Top with a slice of cheese, 2 slices tomato, bacon, and another slice of cheese.
  5. Cover with a slice of bread, butter side out. Fry sandwiches until golden on both sides.
  6. Your sandwitch is ready!!!!!
